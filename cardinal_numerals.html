Why learn the numbers first?
Imagine you are at a pizzeria ordering pizza. You would say to the pizza baker "one Margarita please", and the baker would reply "eight Euro please". Then you would pay and eat your pizza. Only knowing the numbers and "please" is enough to buy food at a restaurant.

You will also learn how to ask someone "How old are you?" as well as how to answer.
